As I was learning new abilities, I realized that I wanted to use my site as a window to help others see the process behind my works, in hopes of inspiring them. On social media, it's easy to feel small and start comparing one's abilities to others' advancements. I aimed to document my learning journey and discuss my works in great detail-- something social media couldn't do meaningfully nor best. It helps to remember that each 'talented' person started from nothing. I believe anyone can develop an ability they didn't think they could before. Every finished complicated-looking product is made up of simple layers/processes -- so, take it step-by-step... then, before you know it, you'll have gotten further than you were yesterday! WOO-HOO PROGRESS!!
